Speed and Innovation — The technological future is laying on a carbon film as thick as an atom: the Graphene. Graphene is a one-atom-thick sheet of carbon atoms arranged in a honeycomb-like pattern which is considered to be the world's thinnest, strongest and most conductive material, to both electricity and heat. It is natural and non-polluting. When touching the skin, it's the first technology that permits a real interaction between body and fabric, dissipating and equalizing the heat in different areas of the body. The Graphene technology has been used for ALE VELOCITY G+ JERSEY.
